Can you imagine the desperation of Big Foot when the Seventh Cavalry found them and huddled them there? The children clinging to the women? The rolling up of those big guns on wheels? The shouted commands? The dangerous, defiant looks of the young men?

Can you imagine the moment of the blast? Pregnant women shot through. The bodies of little boys.

Can you imagine Big Foot, chief of the Miniconjou, cut down and left dead in the snow for days?
